Design and Web Programming Artisan
Hi, Saif, I am a skilled Graphic Designer and Web Developer with a rich background in the dynamic world of chess. Over the past two years, I have honed my expertise while contributing to the All India Chess Federation. My creative journey has been marked by the creation of diverse and impactful designs, including notable projects such as the design work for the 44th FIDE Chess Olympiad. Additionally, I have had the privilege of crafting engaging designs for various national and international chess tournaments. Through my work, I strive to seamlessly blend aesthetics with functionality, delivering visually compelling solutions in the realm of graphic design and web development.
